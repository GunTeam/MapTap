About

This game is about creating a strategy to traverse the map of a Africa through one-country hops. It aims to teach geography in a fun, exploration-based way.

In the game, you play as a trader who travels between countries to peddle goods. Some countries must be avoided (for sales' tax reasons), and some have especially great deals.

Use your knowledge of Africa to end up with the most riches... or get swallowed up by debt!

Learn more about where countries are in Africa while exercising your critical spatial reasoning abilities!
